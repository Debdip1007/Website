Hi, I'm Debdip Guchait.

I'm a physics student interested in quantum optics, quantum network devices, microwave engineering, superconducting circuits,cryogenic Measurement, spin defect centers in large bandgap materials, and experimental physics.

I recently completed a one year research internship at OIST, Japan with the Hybrid Quantum Device team, PI: Yuimaru Kubo, on hybrid devices with spins and superconducting circuits. My MSc is in Physics at IIT Ropar, where I worked on quantum emitters in layered hexagonal boron nitride under Dr. Rajesh V. Nair. Previously, I received Chanakya Fellow from I-Hub IISER Pune and earned my BSc from Midnapore College (Autonomous).
